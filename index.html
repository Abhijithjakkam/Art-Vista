Genre

Painting

Painting is a visual form of expression that allows artists to communicate ideas, emotions, and stories through color, composition, and brushwork. It is a therapeutic and fulfilling creative process that offers endless possibilities for creativity and self-expression.

Impressionism

Impressionism is an artistic movement that emerged in the late 19th century, characterized by capturing fleeting moments and the effects of light through loose brushwork and vibrant colors. It revolutionized the art world, emphasizing the importance of personal perception and evoking emotions through the depiction of everyday subjects.
